The Problem We Noticed

While mental health apps were growing, very few offered an immediate, judgment-free zone combined with access to real certified professionals — especially ones who spoke your language and understood your cultural context.

The Idea

SoulMi was built to bridge that gap — whether you need a 3 AM conversation with an AI that truly listens, or a structured session with a therapist, we created this ecosystem for exactly that moment.

Where We Are Today

A growing sanctuary of over 10,000 members, 20+ certified therapists, and a community that shows up for each other every single day — in Telugu, Hindi, English, and more.

Built from lived experience.

SoulMi was founded by people who understood what it felt like to reach out at 2 AM and find nothing.
